Summary

Purpose:

Why has travel has become such a trend among Millennials? The answer is the amount of information we are able to collect like never before. All the tools available today help us further expand our ambition and go explore first hand. However, there still a lot of work in research that needs to be done to find what we want. One example is finding a good room that fits our budget and comfort. This website will group two popular sites for rooms to give a better overview of what each of the popular cities has to offer today. As a bonus, we added attractions for each of our cities.

Datasources:

Our datasources come from the following sites:

  • Tripadvisor: We used Python splinter to scrape 30 top attractions and 15 pages of results returning 300-400 hotel listings for each city. API was not available.
  • Airbnb: We used Python splinter to scrape 17 pages of results returning ~300 listings for each city. API was not available.
  • Google Search and Image: We used Python splinter to search for city basic information and scrape 10 google images for each attraction per city per season for a total of ~12,000 images. API was not available.
  • mongoDB.Atlas: All scraped data was stored in the cloud version of mongoDB Atlas

    • mongoDB collections:

    DB = city_explorer

  • attraction_details : Top 30 attractions per city
  • city_airbnb : ~300 air bnb listings per city
  • city_tripadvisor : 300-400 hotel listings per city
  • city_info : Overview data of each city
  • city_seasons_photo : Google Image search scraped data
